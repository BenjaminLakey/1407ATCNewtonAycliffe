About the RAF Air Cadets

If you're a fan of aviation, action and adventure, love sports and getting to know people then you're in the right place. Every year nearly 56,000 air cadets and volunteer staff take part in exciting events all over the country. And with over 1,000 squadrons across the UK, you could be one of them.

Looking to Join?

As long as you are over 12 years old and in at least year 8 right up to the age of 17, you are welcome to join us at any time. Why not come down and visit?

20+?

Over 20 and looking to get involved? Why not become a member of Adult Staff or part of our Civilian committee? See our recruitment page for more information.

Where and when?

We parade twice a week on a Monday and a Thursday between 18:45 & 21:30. We are based at the TA Centre in Newton Aycliffe next to the leisure centre. You can find out more details on our Contact Page.
